Contains the interfaces and implementations for {@link com.javadocking.visualizer.Visualizer}s.

Information on using visualizers is in How to Use Visualizers (Minimizers and Maximizers) in The Sanaware Developer Guide.

A visualizer shows a {@link com.javadocking.dockable.Dockable} in a special state. The special state is a constant defined by {@link com.javadocking.dockable.DockableState}, but not {@link com.javadocking.dockable.DockableState#NORMAL} or {@link com.javadocking.dockable.DockableState#CLOSED}.

There are implementations for {@link com.javadocking.dockable.DockableState#MINIMIZED}. These are minimizers:

  • {@link com.javadocking.visualizer.LineMinimizer} shows the minimized dockables in a line at the bottom of a panel.
  • {@link com.javadocking.visualizer.DockingMinimizer} shows the minimized dockables in lines at the borders of a border dock. The minimized dockables can move in their lines, be dragged to other lines, or even be made floating.
There is one implementation for {@link com.javadocking.dockable.DockableState#MAXIMIZED}. This is a maximizer:
  • {@link com.javadocking.visualizer.SingleMaximizer} shows one maximized dockable in a panel.
